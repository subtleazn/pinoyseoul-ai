# PinoySeoul: Company Overview

**Bridging Filipino & Korean Cultures Since 2017**

## Executive Summary

PinoySeoul is an integrated media ecosystem serving the Filipino community in Korea and K-culture enthusiasts in the Philippines. Through our website (PinoySeoul.com) and 24/7 radio streaming platform (Pinoy Seoul Radio), we provide bilingual content, cultural connection, and practical support for Filipinos navigating Korean culture and life in Korea.

---

## Our Purpose

**Mission:**  
To serve as a bridge for Filipinos in Korea and give back to the community through accessible media, cultural content, and practical support that makes their journey easier and more connected.

**Vision:**  
To become the leading Korean-Filipino media platform that strengthens cultural ties, empowers overseas workers, and celebrates the growing relationship between the Philippines and Korea.

---

## What is PinoySeoul?

PinoySeoul is a dual-platform media enterprise:

### 1. PinoySeoul.com — Media Portal & Information Hub

**Content Focus:**
- Latest Korean entertainment news and reviews
- EPS-TOPIK exam resources and study guides
- Living in Korea practical tips and guides
- Community stories and experiences
- Event coverage and cultural features
- Job opportunities and career guidance
- Filipino community support resources

**Language:** Bilingual (English & Filipino)  
**Access:** Free for all users

### 2. Pinoy Seoul Radio — 24/7 Online Streaming Platform

**Programming:**
- K-Pop hits (latest releases and classics)
- K-Drama OST collections
- OPM (Original Pilipino Music) for overseas Filipinos
- Curated playlists for different moods and times
- Live shows and special programs
- Community-driven music selection

**Availability:** Web browser, mobile app  
**Format:** 24/7 continuous streaming

## Our Journey: Timeline

**2017 — Foundation**  
PinoySeoul.com launched to serve Filipinos in Korea. Founded by Nash Ang to give back to the community.

**2018 — EPS-TOPIK Online Community Launch**  
Created EPS-TOPIK Online Facebook group providing free Korean language training for aspiring workers.

**2019 — Cultural Events Era**  
- Korea Philippines Creators Day
- Korea Pinoy International Film Festival (featured "Hello, Love, Goodbye")

**2020 — Radio Launch (COVID-19 Response)**  
Launched Pinoy Seoul Radio as 24-hour streaming platform during pandemic.

**2021-2022 — Platform Integration & Partnerships**  
- Mobile app launch
- Partnerships: Kroma, Wilbros, BENCH, Disney+

**2023-Present — Expansion & Innovation**  
Continuing to strengthen community through offline events and digital content.

## Who We Serve

### Primary Audience Segments

1. **Overseas Filipino Workers (OFWs) in Korea**
   - Filipino workers living and working in South Korea
   - Need: Connection to home, practical life guidance, community support

2. **Aspiring Korea Workers**
   - Filipinos preparing for EPS-TOPIK exam
   - Planning to work in Korea
   - Need: Language learning, exam preparation, employment guidance

3. **K-Culture Enthusiasts in the Philippines**
   - Filipino fans of K-Pop, K-Dramas, and Korean culture
   - Need: Latest news, community connection, cultural content
